And a global survey of birth data showed that more girls are born in the tropics (the region around the equator) than anywhere else in the world. One theory is that weather extremes cause stress. Choosing the right health care provider for pregnancy and childbirth

The family physician (FP) is a doctor who has studied family medicine. This doctor can treat many illnesses and conditions, and treats men and women of all ages. Some family doctors also take care of women who are pregnant. Many will care for you during your pregnancy and when you deliver your baby. Others provide prenatal care only and have an OB or midwife care for you during the birth of your baby. Family medicine doctors may also be trained to take care of your newborn after delivery.
